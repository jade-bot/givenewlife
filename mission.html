New Life Infant Feeding Center is a nurturing community that exists to empower mothers to make sound and confident decisions about the feeding and care of their children by providing:

  • nutrition for mothers and children
  • education and support for breastfeeding
  • awareness of their HIV status and help managing their care
  • education and training on safe birthing options
  • opportunities to advocate for themselves and their children

We believe women can effect positive change the mothering culture of their own countries.

While being respectful to national culture, we will confront misinformation and superstition where it conflicts with known science or interferes with survival and wellness of those we serve.
