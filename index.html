We exist to keep families alive, healthy and together. We focus our work in the poorest areas of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, to address the main issues in their struggle to survive: access to nutritious food, clean water/sanitation and safer birthing options. We long to give these women and children a way out of the deadly and crippling cycle of poverty and empower them to change their lives and their culture for good.
